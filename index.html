About us

Genconian Steel , a CWB certified facility offers one-stop services that include metal fabrication, welding, assembly, and painting. Since the beginning, we have firmly believed that quality, competitive pricing, on-time deliveries, and excellent customer service are all equally important, and strive to be the best source for custom steel fabrication for our customers. We specialize in structural steel, All of our work is performed by professionally-trained welders, fabricators and each job is carefully inspected on-site and must pass our strict quality control procedures before leaving our facility.
